X Ambassadors

About

X Ambassadors is an American rock band that originated in Ithaca, New York, and gained widespread recognition for their dynamic sound, blending elements of rock, pop, and indie. The band consists of lead vocalist Sam Harris, his brother Casey Harris on keyboard, guitarist Noah Feldshuh, and drummer Adam Levin. One of the defining features of X Ambassadors is Sam Harris's soulful and emotive vocals, which add a distinctive and powerful touch to their music. The band's sound is characterized by a fusion of genres, incorporating anthemic choruses, electronic elements, and heartfelt lyrics.

The breakthrough for X Ambassadors came with their hit single "Renegades" in 2015, which climbed the charts and became a commercial success. The song's infectious melody and uplifting lyrics resonated with audiences, propelling the band into the mainstream spotlight. X Ambassadors continued to build on their success with subsequent releases like "Unsteady" and albums like "VHS," showcasing their versatility and ability to craft emotionally charged and memorable music. Beyond their studio work, X Ambassadors are known for their engaging live performances, captivating audiences with their energy and musicianship. With a distinctive sound and a growing discography, X Ambassadors have solidified their place in the contemporary music scene.
